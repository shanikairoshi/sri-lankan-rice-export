Connecting Sri Lankan Rice with International Buyers

Ceylon Rice Global is a starter export catalogue website for presenting selected Sri Lankan rice varieties to international importers, distributors, retailers, restaurants, and food-service buyers.

The website is intentionally designed as a static catalogue first. This allows the business to validate buyer interest before investing in a full backend system, authentication, payments, and order management.
